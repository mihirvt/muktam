Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between M2 & M3 models? +

Our M2 and M3 models represent the state-of-the-art in Text-to-Speech (TTS) & Fine tuned LLM's for telephony and the Indian context.

The M2 model outperforms ElevenLabs' Flash v2.5 and goes head-to-head with their v2 multilingual, in terms of response quality it beats GPT 4o.

Meanwhile, the M3 model rivals ElevenLabs' v3.

Crucially, the M3 model supports over 200+ emotion tags, rendering it completely indistinguishable from human speech.

The response quality is flagship & beats GPT 5.2 in Indian conversational languages.

How fast is the voice response? +

P95 end-to-end latency is sub 800 ms with barge-in support and crosstalk handling.

How fast we can go live? +

Our API's are ready to plug & play, so the time taken for integration & testing will be completely dependent on your team.

Most teams go live within 2 weeks. We’ll work with your team to configure voice agents for your workflows, integrate with your scheduling or web app, and train them on your communication guidelines.

Can it speak multiple languages? +

Yes. We natively support a wide variety of multiple languages out of the box.

How do you ensure accuracy and safety? +

Domain-tuned ASR + policies + tool guards. When uncertain, the agent escalates to a human seamlessly.

Do you integrate with my stack? +

Most likely yes. We offer ready connectors and fast custom builds via API and webhooks.
